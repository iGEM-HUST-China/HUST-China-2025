In the list of core ingredients for high-end cosmetics and personal care products, the premium skincare ingredient squalane has always held a prominent position. And the starting point of it all stems from its precursor—squalene.

Through a single hydrogenation step, squalene is transformed into the well-known skincare star, squalane.

Perfect Moisturization

Squalane quickly penetrates the skin, forming a natural moisture-locking barrier and strengthening the skin barrier, while being suitable for almost all skin types.

Such an ideal ingredient is favored by consumers worldwide.

So, what kind of rapidly growing market is supporting the squalene that fuels its production?

Our Mission

At iGEM HUST-China 2025, we're dedicated to solving the squalene sustainability problem through innovative synthetic biology.

Our project focuses on developing a sustainable production method for squalene by converting waste cooking oil into this valuable cosmetic ingredient.

By engineering microorganisms to efficiently produce squalene, we aim to reduce environmental impact while meeting the growing demand for this important compound.

Technical Innovation

Our approach combines cutting-edge genetic engineering with sustainable resource utilization.

We've developed specialized microbial strains that can efficiently convert waste oil into high-purity squalene through optimized metabolic pathways.

The process reduces production costs by 40% compared to traditional methods while maintaining exceptional product quality.

Environmental Impact

Our solution addresses multiple environmental challenges simultaneously.

By utilizing waste cooking oil as a raw material, we help reduce improper disposal that contributes to water pollution.

Our biotechnological approach eliminates the need for shark harvesting and reduces agricultural land use compared to plant-based alternatives.
