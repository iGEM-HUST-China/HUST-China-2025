Squalene Science

Precursor to Skincare Miracle

Squalene is a naturally occurring lipid compound and the precursor to squalane, widely used in cosmetics.

Its unique molecular structure makes it a prized ingredient in skincare products.

Moisture-Locking Mechanism

Squalane forms a protective barrier on the skin's surface that prevents moisture loss.

This "lock-in rather than soak-in" property makes squalane an exceptional moisturizer.
