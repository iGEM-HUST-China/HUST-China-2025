The Squalene Story

Squalene is a natural lipid compound found in shark liver and some plants.

When hydrogenated, it becomes squalane - a prized ingredient in premium skincare.

Perfect Moisturization

Squalane creates a protective barrier that locks in moisture while allowing skin to breathe.

Exceptional compatibility with all skin types.

Growing Demand

The global squalene market is expanding at 7.5% annually.

Driven by rising demand for natural skincare products.

Shark Liver Source

Traditional but unsustainable source from deep-sea shark livers.

Threatens marine ecosystems and shark populations.

Plant-Based Sources

Sustainable alternatives from olive oil, amaranth, and rice bran.

Renewable but limited by yield and costs.

Biotechnological Production

Modern fermentation technology enables sustainable production.

Engineered microorganisms convert renewable resources.

Our Technology

We've developed a revolutionary process that transforms waste cooking oil into high-purity squalene.

Using advanced synthetic biology and metabolic engineering, we've optimized microbial strains for maximum efficiency.

Our approach reduces production costs by 40% while maintaining exceptional product quality.
